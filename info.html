    About company

    United Water Company LLC is the largest producer of drinking and mineral water, as well as non-alcoholic and soft drinks. The company is engaged in a specially protected ecological resort region Caucasian Mineral Waters. Well No. 79 at the Beshtaugorsky site the field is owned by the United Water Company.

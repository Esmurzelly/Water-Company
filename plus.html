    Production

    The industrial complex is located on the territory of the Caucasian Mineral Waters and is one of the most modern in the region. We have automated equipment for complete cycle from the world's leading manufacturers.

    Main technological advantages:

    Sealed mineral pipeline
    Transportation of mineral water through a closed mineral pipeline straight from the well to the place of production allows you to preserve all the useful properties of water, its purity and primordiality.

    Advanced water treatment technologies
    Minimally affecting the chemical composition and structure of water.

    Using laser marking equipment
    Application of the date of manufacture and shift number allows you to track each unit of production from the operator on the production line to the end user. United Water The company is one of the few manufacturers using laser marking equipment, along with multinational companies.

    Modern electronic equipment technologies provide a high level of automation processes and contribute to the observance of sanitary and hygienic conditions. To ensure sterility filling lines are equipped with automated CIP - washing systems. High quality water the stage of its processing is ensured by the use of the most advanced water treatment technologies.
